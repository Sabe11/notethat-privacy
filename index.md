# NoteThat Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** [Month Day, Year]

## Summary
NoteThat stores your notes locally on your device and, if you sign in, syncs them via Apple iCloud/CloudKit. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

## Data We Collect
- **Apple Sign‑In identifier** (your Apple‑provided user ID) to associate your account.
- **Note content** (text, drawings, metadata) if you enable iCloud sync.
- **Optional images** you import into notes.

We do **not** collect analytics, location data, or contacts.

## How We Use Data
- To provide core functionality (saving and syncing notes).
- To restore data across devices when iCloud sync is enabled.

## Data Storage
- Notes are stored locally on your device.
- If signed in, data is stored in your **private iCloud database** under your Apple ID.

## Data Sharing
We do not share your data with third parties. Apple processes iCloud data under its own privacy policy.

## Data Retention
Data remains until you delete it in the app or turn off iCloud sync.

## Your Choices
- Use the app as a guest (no iCloud sync).
- Sign out to stop syncing.
- Delete notes at any time.
